What is IMPULSE?

Impulse aims to provide students with a world-class technical experience, to inspire and inculcate critical thinking among them by enhancing their problem-solving skills and promote indigenous self-made technology.
Impulse'23 is an event you wouldn't wanna miss! This two-day technical fest will witness young minds in action as they take on these exciting opportunities that are sure to prove once in a lifetime!

Past Events

4
2022

IMPULSE'22 was the third fest of a tech-fest series, and the first offline event in college after COVID-19. It included various events like HackMAIT 3.0, Web Wizards, Sudo Solve, BGMI, and HourGlass.

2019

IMPULSE'19 was the second version of a tech-fest series that included events like Algorythm, PubG, CS:Go, DevDash, and Explora. The most special event was HackMAIT 2.0, a 36-hour hackathon where winners received prizes worth 1 lac.

2018

Impulse'18 was the first tech fest of IOSD-MAIT, which included events like PIXEL, CodeSaga, and a game development workshop. It also organized the first version of HackMAIT, a 36-hour hackathon where winners received prizes worth 1 lac.
