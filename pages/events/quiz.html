Quizo-Rama

Quiz

Welcome to Quizo-Rama, a five-round quiz event designed to test your knowledge and challenge your intellect. Compete against other trivia enthusiasts in a battle of wits to see who will come out on top. With a variety of categories and questions, this is the ultimate test of your mental acuity.

Quizo-Rama is a fun and engaging event that challenges your knowledge, memory, and mental agility. It's perfect for team building, parties, or just a night of entertainment with friends and family. So get ready to put your thinking caps on and see who will come out on top in the ultimate battle of wits.

The event will consist of 2 rounds:

  • Round 1(Preliminary Round): Participants are required to answer 20 questions in 45 minutes. All participants are required to attend the quiz at Maharaja Agrasen Insititute of Technology, Rohini, Delhi.
  • Round 2 (Final Round): The Final Round will consist of multiple rounds. The shortlisted teams will compete for glory. Meet the brightest minds of the quizzing circuit and get ready to go on an adventurous journey. Details of round formats will be disclosed during the event. Participants are required to be present at the campus

Rules

Rule Book

  • Each team will consist of a maximum of two players.
  • Only the Team Representative/Leader shall fill the form.
  • There is no restriction on whether the team members are from the same organization/college or not.
  • Players must register in advance and provide their team’s name.
  • The quizmaster will read out the questions, and teams will answer on the spot.
  • All the event-specific rules and guidelines will be conveyed before the event
  • In the case of a tiebreaker, more questions will be asked.
  • Cheating or disruptive behaviour will result in disqualification.
  • The quizmaster's decision is final in all matters related to the event.
  • The use of electronic devices, including phones, is prohibited during the event
  • The event will begin promptly at the designated start time. Latecomers may not be allowed to participate.
  • Participants must be 18 years or older.
  • The event organizers reserve the right to modify the event format or rules at any time.
